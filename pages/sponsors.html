Supporting The Future Of Women In Stem

Women in STEM continue to shape the future of New Zealand and the world through innovation, research, technology, healthcare, engineering, and scientific discovery. From developing life-changing technologies to solving global environmental challenges, women bring valuable perspectives and talent to every part of the STEM industry.


Yet despite their contributions, many women still face barriers in accessing opportunities, funding, mentorship, and representation within STEM fields. In Waikato and across New Zealand, countless talented young women have the potential to succeed — but they need support to turn ambition into achievement.


By becoming a sponsor, you can help create scholarships, learning opportunities, networking programs, and career pathways for the next generation of women in STEM. Your support is more than a donation — it is an investment in innovation, diversity, and the future of our communities.


Be the reason more women in Waikato pursue STEM. Be the reason more women succeed in STEM. Sponsor the future today.
