About Us

Shaping the Future of STEM, One woman at a time

Waikato Women in STEM is a student-led community at the University of Waikato dedicated to supporting and empowering women pursuing studies in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). The club brings together students who identify as women from diverse STEM disciplines to build a supportive network that promotes academic success, professional development, and confidence in traditionally underrepresented fields.


Through workshops, networking events, mentoring opportunities, and social activities, the club aims to create an inclusive environment where members can share knowledge, develop skills, and connect with peers, researchers, and industry professionals. These initiatives help foster collaboration, encourage leadership, and inspire the next generation of women innovators and problem-solvers.


By cultivating a strong community and providing opportunities for growth, Waikato Women in STEM seeks to increase representation, strengthen career pathways, and empower women to contribute meaningfully to the future of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
